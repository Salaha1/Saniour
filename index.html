Our Story

Al Saniour has always been committed to achieve the highest standards of excellence in delivering an exquisite taste for the palate and maintaining high levels of personal care and service for full guest satisfaction.

We started from a small place in Dhour Shweir back in 1993 and started growing as the days went by to become a four branch chain in Lebanon, marking Dhour Shweir, Antelias, Sarba and Sahel Alma on the local map.
